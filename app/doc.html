Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Shapiro + Raj (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you visit our website, request a briefing, or otherwise interact with our services. By using the site, you agree to the practices described below. For related terms, see our Terms of Use.

Information we collect

We may collect information that you provide directly, such as your name, company, job title, email address, phone number, and the content of messages you send through contact or briefing forms. We also automatically collect certain technical data when you use the site, including IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages viewed, and referring URLs, through cookies and similar technologies where permitted.

Examples of data we may process

  • Identity and contact: name, employer, role, email, phone.
  • Communications: messages you send via forms or email.
  • Technical: IP address, device/browser data, cookies; see also Cookies.

If you exercise privacy rights

Typical steps when you contact us about your data:

  1. Send a request to communications@shapiroraj.com with enough detail for us to verify your identity.
  2. We confirm receipt and may ask for additional information only as needed to process your request.
  3. We respond within the timeframe required by applicable law, or explain any extension where permitted.

Subprocessors and vendors

We rely on a limited set of service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, email). They process data only on our instructions and under appropriate agreements.

Regional notices

Residents of certain states or countries may have additional rights; nothing in this policy limits those rights where they apply.

Document version

This page may be archived as PDF; the online version controls in case of discrepancy.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, schedule briefings, operate and improve our website and services, analyze usage trends, communicate with you about relevant offerings (where you have not opted out where required), comply with legal obligations, and protect our rights and the security of our users.

Sharing of information

We may share information with service providers who assist us with hosting, analytics, email delivery, or other business functions, subject to confidentiality obligations. We may disclose information if required by law, in connection with a legal process, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of Shapiro + Raj, our clients, or others. We do not sell your personal information as that term is commonly understood.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and similar tools to remember preferences, understand how visitors use the site, and measure campaign performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect certain features of the site.

Data retention and security

We retain information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information; no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by following the instructions in those messages.

International transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate. We take steps intended to ensure appropriate safeguards where required by applicable law.

Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date. Your continued use of the site after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy, to the extent permitted by law.

Contact us
